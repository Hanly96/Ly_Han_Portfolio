Brand Characteristic

Just like any thriving community or sports team, there’s typically an influential leader behind every successful brand. For large companies, this may be the CEO. For smaller ones, it’s usually the owner.

Therefore, I choose image of cat to express the fast movement, its smartness, activeness, aggression and competitiveness.

Additionally, basketball teamwork is in fact very important as it allows the team to function together and not individually. The basketball icon can represent the strength of team work that makes the logo will be powerful.
