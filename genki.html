Brand Characteristic

We take the inspiration from the protection of environment. Planet has suffered from the serious pollution with the tons of plastic and the material that cannot recycle. The idea of using the sustainable materials to create this infuser bottle. The two main ingredient of this would be wood and glass. Wood is a versatile raw material and the only renewable construction material. Glass is a sustainable, fully recyclable material which provides great environmental benefits such as contributing to mitigating climate change and saving precious natural resources.

Our goal is that selling this product in the community as soon as it gets popular and become a no.1 quality and most choose able product.
